100 million contacts

There are tens of millions of contacts in DayBreak’s Key Decision Maker database. This number is growing by tens of thousands of contacts each day.

DayBreak maintains a vast server network that works in a similar fashion to the way Google indexes the internet. However, DayBreak’s spiders focus on corporate, educational and governmental websites to capture all of the names, job titles and addresses of key business executives within each organization.

Additionally, DayBreak’s spiders “read” every major newspaper each day to identify job promotions, demotions and changes in corporate hierarchy. All of these names, and job titles are then compared to our master database. New names are added, job titles are changed, addresses are adjusted accordingly and ultimately, we have the DayBreak Key Decision Maker Database.

The scope of Daybreak’s world-class contact directory is unrivaled. DayBreak helps companies find and reach decision makers. We provide the ultimate tools for getting in touch with business leaders throughout the world. We leverage our contact list to raise your company profile to new and existing clients, introduce new products/services/technologies to the market, and generate leads to meet your goals. Unique to DayBreak is the fact that we have websites and email addresses for everyone of our key decision makers. Our email addresses have the astounding delivery rate of 97%.

400 industries

DayBreak has one of the largest and most qualified list of key decision makers in the world. We’ve got over three million websites, and corporate information profiled. Whether you are looking for those within the Fortune 1000, Global 500, Asian 500, medium sized corporations, or small to medium sized accounts – we can provide you with the best list of contacts available today.

DayBreak has over a million key decision makers for small businesses. Our definition of small business is less than twenty-five employees. We have their website addresses, email addresses, and telephone numbers. You can search these small businesses by SIC code, keywords, company name and a number of other fields.

We have extensive contacts in the pharmaceutical industry. If you are looking for individual contacts that are not in our database, we can find them for you through our Contact Concierge program.

DayBreak has the best list of key decision makers within the SMB accounts available today. These include the General Managers, Presidents, Chief Executive Officers, Sales Managers and other key decision makers responsible for 25 to 250 employees.

7 million organizations

We have roughly 7 million organizations in our databases.

The ideal organizations are those that a) have a website presence, b) their website is professional, c) have a respectable good, product or service, d) could be a potential marketing partner,and e) could be a potential purchaser of our subscribers' goods, products or services. DayBreak has built one of the most extensive contact lists available for City Government category in the United States. We focused the largest cities in the US. We began our investigative work by going to each of these municipal governments’ websites and starting with the Mayor’s office, we quickly identified the Chief of Police, Captain of the Fire Department, District Attorney’s office and all of the primary offices within each respective city.
We have almost every school, junior college, college and university in the United States in our contact list database. The contacts in our database include any key decision makers on the campus. This includes those at the highest level such as Chancellors of University, Deans of Colleges, Principals at high school, middle school or grade schools. We also have any other individual that controls a budget within the educational system. For example, we have focused on identifying and profiling those responsible for computer hardware and software, copy machines, and supplies for the school. We’ve also focused on those responsible for sports who are responsible for purchasing football, basketball, and sports training equipment. We have also focused on the medical profession, the doctors, nurses and other individuals who are responsible for the healthcare needs of the people.

3 million companies

DayBreak has approximately 3 million qualified companies in its Key Decision Maker database.

We provide a great deal of information for organizations. This includes a) number of total employees, b) number of employees at location, c) revenues, d) SIC code, e) keywords associated with organization, f) category 1 – primary, g) category 2 – subcategory, h) category 3 – drilling down on the subcategory, and i) category 4 and 5 - where we drill down even further on the categories and subcategories.

DayBreak’s database was built over the past twenty years. Our CEO and Founder, Doug Monahan started a sales company called Sunset Direct in 1992. The database was built by over 350 sales people making outbound telephone calls into the database for over 15 years. We’ve also developed sophisticated spiders to visit web sites and capture the key decision makers from the executive management team section as well as from press releases on the site.

We also visit trade show websites to obtain lists of keynote speakers, break out section speakers, and other influential individuals.
